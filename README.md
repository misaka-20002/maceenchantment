# MaceEnchantment


This mod adds a Mace Smash enchantment which enables everything(swords,axes) has "Mace" effect.

It provides Mace Smash I-IV with each level dealing lvl/4 * 100% damage of the full damage.

And it has ability to "grow" while you add more other mods!

The damage curve has two types: one is minecraft original mace&density damage curve, another one is scale the mace damage with 0.1x your current damage and density damage with 0.05x your current damage (replace the original 0.5)

So you wont get soooooo low damage while other mods give you powerful weapons :)
